About Me

I am an Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering student with a passion for merging technology and innovation. My journey began with a fascination for hardware systems and evolved into a love for software development. This blend has driven me to work on projects that integrate both fields, such as my Smart Traffic Management System, where I combine IoT and Machine Learning to solve real-world problems.

Currently, I’m focused on learning backend development, particularly the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js). I’m also exploring UI/UX design, with the aim of creating intuitive and user-friendly web applications. My front-end development experience includes building responsive, modern websites, such as the one for Star Cosmetics, where I focus on delivering clean, interactive designs that offer a great user experience.
